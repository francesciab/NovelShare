I Made a Fortune by Marrying A Sickly Woman!

Author: Fan Que

Chapter 1: Twin sisters

Huizhou Prefecture, She County. At night, the streets and alleys are quiet, and you can occasionally hear a few dogs barking. A group of officials suddenly broke into the Song family's home. The leading official claimed that the medical materials sold by the Song family to the Northwest Army were inferior and had resulted in deaths. They were ordered to search the family's home and investigate the crime.

"Oh no! There's a flood in the backyard!" "Go put out the fire! The eldest and second young ladies are still in the house!" "Rescue!" The Song family was in chaos and there were cries everywhere. An ordinary horse-drawn carriage came out of the alley next to the Song residence and hurried towards the city gate. Inside the carriage, Song Jin hugged her half sister Song Xiu. Even though he was extremely frightened in his heart, his face remained calm.

The old man who was driving the car said, "Miss, when you get to Qinjiaou, tell everyone that you are from the next county and both your parents are dead."
